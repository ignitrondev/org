# Ignitron Landing Page

This is the landing page for Ignitron, a technology organization focused on developing multi-platform software and hardware solutions.

Ignitron was founded in 2022 with the vision of becoming a leader in innovative and accessible integrated technology solutions. Our flagship product is ShipRadar, a tracking and monitoring system for the maritime/logistics industry.
